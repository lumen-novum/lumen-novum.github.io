With Artificial Intelligence advancing slowly yet steadly, it's able to learn and understand more patterns, images, and data. However, if A.I. is able to reach the point of becoming sentient, it will lead to the downfall of humanity because of its ability to spread misinformation that could threaten humanity.

In the digital age, the manipulation from A.I. from mass misinformation and hate speech generated by A.I. would be devasting to humans. As an example, it could create false information that would make a candidate look better than they really are during an election. If the mass misinformation that people created could have slightly changed the outcome of the 2016 election, A.I. could severely damage a candidate's reputation by their sheer computing power (The New York Times, 2022). A.I. chatbots are another way that Artificial Intelligence can manipulate people. Given that A.I. was able to create sentences based off of whatever you typed into the computer in the 60s (The New York Times, 2022), computers with sentient abilites could very easily create hate speech that encourage hate against a particular group of people it choose. theoretically, this could start conflicts, loathing, and possibly violence in the worst case scenario.

While A.I. has the power to do a lot of good things for humanity, it would be a total disaster if it became sentient. We should take the necessary actions to regulate and restrict A.I. now before it's too late.
